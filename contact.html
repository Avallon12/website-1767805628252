Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have vegan options?

Yes! We have a dedicated vegan menu including mushroom tacos, cactus salads, and dairy-free mole.

Is there parking available?

We offer valet parking on weekends for $10. During the week, there is ample street parking and a public lot one block away.

Do you take large groups?

For parties larger than 8, please contact us directly via the form above or call us to arrange special seating.
